## 一、IVL 物理機制

### 1.1 EHL 技術基礎
- IVL 基於 electrohydraulic lithotripsy (EHL)，即 spark gap 放電產生聲波壓力波 [p.2]
- Spark gap 放電 → 形成 acoustic pressure wave + 隨後的 plasma vapor bubble (cavitation bubble) [p.2]
- 標準 EHL shockwave pressure pulse 持續約 5–10 ms，由近瞬時 peak positive component（壓縮應力）+ 較長 negative pressure trough（拉伸應力）組成 [p.2]

### 1.2 ESWL vs ISWL vs IVL 參數對比（Table 1, p.4）
| Parameter | ESWL | ISWL | IVL |
|-----------|------|------|-----|
| Emitter location | Extracorporeal | Intracorporeal | Intravascular |
| Focused/Unfocused | Focused | Unfocused | Unfocused |
| Peak positive pressure (MPa) | 30–110 | 3–6 | ~5 |
| Peak negative pressure (MPa) | 8–15 | 1–4 | 0.3 |
| Energy flux density (mJ/mm²) | 0.1–3.0 | — | ~0.0086 |
| Penetration depth (mm) | ~150 | ~10 | 3–7 |
| Primary fracture mechanism | Compression + tensile stress, cavitation, spall, squeezing, superfocusing | Compression + tensile stress, cavitation | Compression stress |

### 1.3 IVL 聲波波形修改（三大關鍵適應性改造）
1. **低正壓 + 極低負壓**：IVL positive peak pressure ~5 MPa (~50 atm)；negative peak pressure ~0.3 MPa (~3 atm)，遠低於組織損傷閾值 [p.4]
2. **Emitters 封裝在 balloon 內**：以液體緩衝熱傷害 [p.4]
3. **多 emitters 沿導管串聯排列**：實現縱向治療 [p.4]

### 1.4 能量細節
- IVL 聲波以 unfocused 方式 circumferentially and transmurally 輻射 [p.4]
- Energy flux density 比 ESWL 低數個數量級 [p.4]
- IVL 目標是 calcium fracture（非 pulverization），因 emitter 近距離啟動，所需能量遠低於 ESWL [p.4]
- 每個 pulse 持續 0.6–1.2 ms，速率 1 Hz (1 pulse/s) [p.13]
- 每 pulse 能量極低：8–10 mJ，且衰減快速 [p.13]

### 1.5 聲阻抗（Acoustic Impedance）原理
- 水 1.5 × 10⁶ kg/(m²·s)、腎臟 1.6 × 10⁶、肌肉 1.7 × 10⁶ → 軟組織間聲阻抗相近，shockwave 通過時影響極小 [p.2]
- 骨 / calcified tissue 聲阻抗 7.8 × 10⁶ → 與軟組織差異大，shockwave 遇到鈣化時產生碎裂效應 [p.2]
- IVL shockwave 傳過軟組織 with minimal effect，遇到 calcium 時 leading edge 施加 compressive stress → primary mechanism of calcium fracture [p.4]

## 二、Balloon 構造與材料

### 2.1 IVL 導管整體構造
- Single-use, sterile, disposable catheter，shaft 上含 multiple spark gap–based lithotripsy emitters，整合在 balloon 內 [p.4]
- 經 0.014-inch guidewire 引入目標血管 [p.4]
- Balloon 以 50:50 saline/contrast mixture 充填（離子為 spark generation 所需）[p.4]
- Balloon 充至 subnominal pressure 4 atm 以提供 vessel wall apposition [p.4]

### 2.2 Integrated Balloon 的優勢（三大功能）
1. **散熱與屏蔽**：saline/contrast mixture 散除 vapor bubble 產生的熱量；屏蔽 emitters 避免直接接觸血管壁 [p.4]
2. **Periodic deflation**：pulse delivery cycles 間定期洩氣，散熱 + 移除殘餘 gas bubbles + 允許組織再灌流以減少缺血 [p.5]
3. **機械穩定**：提供支撐，減少治療期間的組織變形 [p.5]
4. **聲學透明**：balloon 薄且 acoustically transparent，提供有效的 fluid-to-tissue transmission [p.5]

### 2.3 Balloon 為 semi-compliant balloon [p.4, p.15]
- IVL 治療時 balloon 僅充至 4 atm（subnominal），治療後充至 6 atm prior to deflation [p.3, Figure 1 legend]
- Semi-compliant balloon 結合 emitters on the shaft [p.4]

### 2.4 三款 IVL 導管規格（Table 2, p.5）
| Spec | Shockwave M5 | Shockwave S4 | Shockwave C2 |
|------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| 用途 | Peripheral | Peripheral | Coronary |
| 直徑 (mm) | 3.5–7.0 | 2.5–4.0 | 2.5–4.0 |
| Sheath | 6F (3.5–6.0 mm), 7F (6.5–7.0 mm) | 5F | 6F (guide catheter) |
| Guidewire | 0.014 inch | 0.014 inch | 0.014 inch |
| Balloon 長度 (mm) | 60 | 40 | 12 |
| Working length (cm) | 110 | 135 | 138 |
| Crossing profile (inches) | 0.054–0.073 | 0.048–0.050 | 0.044–0.047 |
| Pulse frequency | 1 pulse/s | 1 pulse/s | 1 pulse/s |
| Max pulses/catheter | 300 | 160 | 80 |
| Channels | 3 | 2 | 1 |

### 2.5 Emitter 排列
- Emitters 位於 guidewire lumen 外，沿 balloon segment 串聯排列 [p.5]
- 每對 emitters 呈 diametrically opposing pairs（180° 對稱），產生近對稱的球形能量分佈 [p.5]
- Channels 各以 1 Hz pulsing [p.5]
- 中心 channel（如 M5）僅單一 emitter，故 peak acoustic pressure 較兩側 channels 高 [p.5]

## 三、安全性機制：為何 IVL 不傷害軟組織

### 3.1 聲波物理學保護
- **低正壓力**：IVL ~5 MPa vs ESWL 30–110 MPa [p.4, Table 1]
- **極低負壓力**：IVL ~0.3 MPa vs ESWL 8–15 MPa → 負壓遠低於 tissue damage threshold → mitigating cavitation-induced injury [p.4]
- **低 energy flux density**：IVL ~0.0086 mJ/mm² vs ESWL 0.1–3.0 mJ/mm²（低數個數量級）[p.4, Table 1]
- **Unfocused delivery**：不聚焦，能量自然隨距離衰減 [p.4]
- **淺穿透深度**：IVL 3–7 mm vs ESWL ~150 mm [p.4, Table 1]

### 3.2 Balloon 提供的保護
- Balloon 封裝 emitters → 避免 direct contact with vessel wall [p.4]
- 50:50 saline/contrast mixture 散熱（dissipate heat from vapor bubble formation）[p.4]
- Periodic deflation 移除殘餘 gas bubbles + 散熱 [p.5]
- Balloon 提供機械穩定，減少組織變形 [p.5]

### 3.3 與 ESWL 軟組織損傷的對比
- ESWL 的 cavitation（microjets + heat generation）可導致：腹壁夾層、結腸穿孔、肝血腫、腎血管嚴重破壞 [p.3]
- ISWL 安全邊際較窄，因 emitter + cavitation bubbles 直接暴露於周圍組織 → 穿孔、出血、黏膜剝落風險 [p.3]
- IVL 的 negligible negative pressures + balloon integration → 大幅降低 cavitation bubble–induced injury 風險 [p.6]

### 3.4 與 Ultrasound 的比較
- Diagnostic/therapeutic ultrasound: 1–12 MHz, 等正負壓幅度 → 吸收能量→ 產生熱 → tissue ablation [p.6]
- IVL shockwaves 無類似頻率或正弦振盪 → thermal injury 風險更低 [p.6]

### 3.5 Porcine 動物實驗驗證
- 獨立實驗室比較 IVL（180 pulses, 6 cycles, 4 atm）vs 單純 balloon angioplasty 對 porcine iliac and femoral arteries 的影響 [p.12]
- 28 天後 histomorphometric analysis：tissue injury, inflammation, fibrin deposition, endothelialization, neointimal smooth muscle content → **所有參數兩組無差異** [p.12]
- 周圍肌肉組織未發現異常 → IVL 治療影響僅限於血管壁 [p.12]

## 四、Balloon Rupture 的物理學解釋

### 4.1 文中未專門討論 balloon rupture
- 本文**未包含 balloon rupture 作為獨立主題**的討論
- 未提及 balloon rupture 的發生率、機制或風險因素
- 文中僅提到 balloon 設計功能（semi-compliant, periodic deflation, low-pressure inflation at 4 atm）的保護機制

### 4.2 相關物理背景（間接推論材料）
- IVL balloon 內 spark gap 放電產生 vapor bubble，balloon 需承受此 bubble 的衝擊 [p.4]
- Balloon 充填 50:50 saline/contrast mixture；vapor bubble formation 被 balloon 所 contain [p.3, Figure 1 legend: "vapor bubble formation is contained within the integrated balloon"]
- Crossing profile 因 EHL emitters 整合而大於標準 angioplasty balloon catheters [p.15]

## 五、Preclinical 安全性數據

### 5.1 Ultracal 30 Gypsum Ring 體外實驗
- Ultracal 30 gypsum rings 模擬 in vivo 鈣化組織的聲學特性 [p.5]
- 當 IVL balloon 適當 sizing 確保 balloon-wall apposition 時，觀察到更大程度的 ring fracturing [p.5]
- 4 atm subnominal pressure 即可達到足夠 balloon-vessel wall apposition [p.5]

### 5.2 屍體研究（CVPath Institute）
- Fresh-frozen human cadaveric femoral arteries (n = 6) with intimal and medial calcium [p.7]
- Shockwave IVL 60-mm catheter, 5.5–7.0 mm balloons [p.7]
- 180 pulses at 4 atm, 1 pulse/s, 30 pulses/cycle, 6 cycles, 10-s deflation between cycles [p.7]
- **Micro-CT 結果**：circumferential, transverse, and longitudinal calcium fractures，涉及 superficial and deep layers [p.7, Figure 3]
- **Histopathology 結果**：co-registration 確認 multiple full-thickness + deep calcium fractures（未穿透至管腔表面）；high-magnification 顯示 superficial and deep layers 均有 microfractures [p.8, Figures 4 & 5]

### 5.3 Porcine 動物研究
- 如上 §3.5：28-day histomorphometric analysis 顯示 IVL vs balloon alone 所有參數無差異 [p.12]
- 周圍肌肉組織無異常 [p.12]

### 6.1 血管併發症（極低發生率）

| Complication | IVL | Directional Atherectomy | Rotational Atherectomy | Orbital Atherectomy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Distal embolization | 0.0% | 2.3% | 8.0% | 2.2% |
| Dissection (D–F) | 0.9% | 0.8% | NR | NR |
| Perforation | 0.3% | 2.3% | NR | 0.7% |
| Abrupt closure | 0.0% | NR | NR | 1.5% |
| Slow flow | 0.0% | NR | NR | 4.4% |

#### Coronary IVL — Disrupt CAD I/II/III/IV [p.13–14, Table 4]
| Complication | IVL | Rotational Atherectomy | Orbital Atherectomy | Laser Atherectomy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| In-hospital MI | 5.0–6.8% | 2.0% | 9.3% | 2.1% |
| Dissection (D–F) | 0.0–0.3% | 3.0% | 0.9% | 5.3% |
| Perforation | 0.0–0.3% | 4.0% | 0.9% | 0.0% |
| Abrupt closure | 0.3% | NR | 0.2% | 0.0% |
| Slow flow | 0.0% | 2.0% | 0.5% | 0.0% |
| No reflow | 0.0% | — | 0.0% | — |

- Disrupt CAD III: **no perforations, abrupt closures, or no-reflow events** following IVL alone [p.13]
- No distal embolization, no-reflow, or abrupt closure in Disrupt PAD III interim analysis [p.12]

### 6.2 IVL-Induced Capture（心室奪取）
- **機制**：mechano-electric coupling — acoustic pressure waves 經由 cardiac stretch-activated channels 與傳導系統耦合 [p.13]
- **條件**：primarily 發生於 resting heart rate < 60 bpm（R-R interval > 1 s）[p.13]
- **表現**：sustained capture at 60 bpm for duration of IVL treatment (~10 s)；可為 single atrial, single ventricular, or sustained ventricular capture [p.13]
- **Disrupt CAD III 發生率**：41.1% of cases [p.14]
- **臨床意義**：benign, 無 sustained ventricular arrhythmias，無 adverse events [p.14]
- **獨立預測因子**（multivariate Cox regression）：heart rate ≤60 bpm, male sex, total number of IVL pulses delivered [p.14]
- Heart rate <65 bpm 的患者發生 IVL-induced capture 的機率為 ≥65 bpm 者的 **16 倍**（OR 16.3; 95% CI 2.4–110.8; p = 0.004）[p.13–14]

### 6.3 VF（心室顫動）風險
- IVL 每 pulse 8 mJ（分佈在 C2 balloon 表面），遠低於 ICD testing 所需的 0.6–2.0 J → VF/VT 誘發風險極低 [p.13]
- 一例個案報告提示 coronary IVL 與 VF 相關，但被 confound by：balloon inflation-induced ischemia、spontaneous ventricular ectopy、off-label indication [p.13]
- VF 是 coronary procedures 的已知風險，特別是 myocardial ischemia 存在時 [p.13]

### 6.4 Perforation 風險的解釋
- 儘管 deep calcium fractures (to adventitia) 已被觀察到，但 Disrupt CAD 及 Disrupt PAD 系列中 **IVL 單獨使用未見 vessel perforation** [p.10]
- 風險被以下因素減輕：(1) IVL acoustic shockwaves 穿過軟組織 with minimal effect; (2) adventitial fibrosis 在晚期冠狀及周邊動脈疾病中普遍存在 [p.10]

### 6.5 無 Distal Embolization
- IVL 是 balloon-based → atheromatous embolization 風險可能低於 debulking atheroablative devices [p.12]
- Atherectomy 產生 microparticulate debris → embolize → slow/no reflow → ischemia/infarction [p.12]
- IVL 產生的 larger calcium fragments 留在原位 (remain in situ) [p.12]

## 七、與其他 Calcium Modification 技術的比較

### 7.1 IVL vs 高壓球囊（Noncompliant / Cutting / Scoring Balloons）
- 高壓球囊依賴 **high static pressure** 修飾斑塊 → barotrauma 風險 [p.15]
- IVL 使用 acoustic shockwaves，balloon 僅充至 **4 atm** → 避免高壓充氣的 barotrauma [p.15]
- High-pressure balloon dilatation 可能力量不足以 fracture calcium，且可導致 dissection or perforation [p.1]

### 7.2 IVL vs Atheroablative Technologies（Rotational / Orbital Atherectomy）
- Atherectomy 依賴 **localized debulking of superficial calcium** → thermal injury + vascular complications [p.15]
- **Wire bias** → eccentric ruts/troughs → incomplete calcium modification [p.1, p.15]
- Atherectomy 對 **deep calcium** 修飾能力有限（除非 marked wire bias 或 larger device sizes，兩者均影響安全）[p.15]
- IVL fractures **both superficial and deep calcium in situ** → 最低化 vascular complications 和 thermal injury [p.15]

### 7.3 IVL vs Laser Atherectomy
- Laser atherectomy 效果 "remains unpredictable" [p.1]

### 7.4 IVL 獨特優勢總結
- Balloon-based delivery → 低 atheromatous embolization 風險 [p.12]
- Low-pressure (4 atm) → 低 barotrauma [p.15]
- Circumferential + transmural effect → 不受 wire bias 影響 [p.4]
- Treats superficial + deep calcium [p.7–8]
- Minimal learning curve（Disrupt CAD III: roll-in vs pivotal cases 無差異）[p.14]

## 八、IVL Calcium Fracture 機制摘要

### 8.1 Fracture 類型
- Micro-CT 與 histopathology 確認：circumferential, transverse, and longitudinal calcium fractures [p.7]
- 涉及 superficial and deep layers [p.7–8]
- Full-thickness fractures + deep calcium fractures 未穿透至管腔表面 [p.8]
- Microfractures 在 high magnification 下可見 [p.8]

### 8.2 OCT 影像驗證
- Disrupt CAD I/II/III OCT substudies + Disrupt PAD II OCT subanalysis 一致顯示 [p.8]：
  1. Luminal gain 機制 = calcium fracture（無需 high-pressure balloon dilatation）
  2. Circumferential and longitudinal calcium fracture
  3. IVL 改善 vessel compliance → 促進 stent expansion
- Disrupt CAD III: calcium fracture observed in 67.7% of lesions after IVL [p.10]
- 無 fracture 可見的病灶可能有 "out of plane" fractures 或超出 OCT 解析度的 microfractures [p.10]

### 8.3 Balloon Sizing 的重要性
- Disrupt PAD II post hoc analysis: optimal technique = correct balloon sizing (IVL balloon/RVD ≥ 1) + avoidance of therapeutic miss [p.6]
- Optimal technique → significant improvement in 12-month primary patency + reduction in TLR [p.6]
- IVL balloon size > RVD 時**未增加 adverse events** [p.6]
- Recommended sizing: M5/S4 → 1.1:1 with target vessel RVD; C2 → 1:1 [p.6]
