## Case Summary

### Patient
- 82 歲女性，主訴 exertional chest pain，診斷 stable angina [p.1]
- 日常生活獨立（independent in ADL）[p.1]
- 既往史：hypertension、dyslipidemia、paroxysmal atrial fibrillation [p.1]
- 用藥：amlodipine 5 mg、atorvastatin 5 mg、edoxaban 30 mg [p.1]

### Investigations
- 理學檢查、抽血、TTE 均 unremarkable；ECG 無急性缺血 [p.2]
- Diagnostic coronary angiography：mid-LAD severe stenosis with heavy calcification [p.2]
- Second diagonal branch (D2) chronic total occlusion，但有 LCX 良好側枝循環（well-collateralized）[p.2]
- LAD 被選為唯一 revascularization target [p.2]

### Lesion & IVL Details
- PCI approach：left radial，7-F guiding catheter（Launcher EBU 3.5, Medtronic）[p.2]
- Guidewire：SION blue (Asahi Intecc) [p.2]
- Preprocedural OCT 確認 severe circumferential calcification [p.2]
- IVL balloon：2.5 × 12 mm Shockwave C2 (Shockwave Medical) [p.2]
- 在 pulse delivery 過程中 balloon rupture 發生 [p.2]

## Balloon Rupture Event

### Detection
- Indeflator pressure abruptly dropped → 辨識為 balloon rupture [p.4]
- Fluoroscopy 下未見 balloon contents（contrast or air）漏入 coronary lumen [p.4]
- Retrieved balloon 確認為 **pin-hole tear** [p.4]

### Immediate Response
- 停止 inflation、fully deflated balloon、退入 guiding catheter [p.4-5]
- 確認 hemodynamic stability、無 ischemic signs（症狀或 ECG 變化）[p.4-5]
- Minimal contrast injection 排除 perforation 及 slow-flow/no-reflow [p.5]
- 初始 angiography 未見 overt perforation 或 dissection [p.1, p.3]

---

## Retrograde Dissection — 事件經過

### Trigger
- 後續為進行 OCT imaging 而注入 contrast（10 mL at 3 mL/s）→ 觸發 extensive coronary dissection，起源於 rupture site [p.3]

### Bailout Management
- Drug-eluting stent deployed from LAD dissection entry to LM（seal the entry point）[p.3]
- Final angiography 發現 distal LCX 出現 suspicious "to-and-fro" flow pattern [p.3]
- IVUS 檢查揭示：extensive intramural hematoma (IMH) propagating from LM to distal LCX [p.3]

### Conservative Decision
- 儘管 dissection 範圍廣泛，LCX 維持 TIMI flow grade 3 [p.3]
- 選擇 conservative management 而非 additional stenting [p.3]

## Mechanistic Discussion

### 1. Balloon Rupture 機轉 — Thermal Injury [p.3-4]

- 傳統 compliant balloon rupture：complex heavily calcified lesions 且/或 inflation pressure exceeds rated burst pressure [p.3]
- IVL balloon 在 low pressure 下 pulse delivery → rupture 機制不同於傳統 balloon [p.3]
- **Asymmetric balloon expansion**：vessel tortuosity + eccentric calcification → IVL emitter 被迫接近或直接接觸 balloon material → thermal injury → rupture during pulse emission [p.3-4]
- 本案特點：**即使沒有 severe tortuosity**，eccentric (noncircumferential) calcification 單獨就可導致 minor deviation in balloon expansion，sufficient to compromise emitter-balloon wall clearance → thermal injury [p.4]
- 作者強調需用 **high-frame-rate cine acquisition**（而非 simple fluoroscopic storage）來驗證 symmetric balloon expansion 並偵測 subtle deformations [p.4]
- Pin-hole tear 的危害：high-velocity jet from rupture point → focal vascular injury → coronary dissection (including IMH) or perforation [p.4-5]

### 2. Dissection Propagation 方向 — "Path of Least Resistance" [p.5-6]

作者區分 **trigger**（製造 entry）vs **propagation determinants**（wall integrity + geometric steering）：

#### Trigger
- IVL balloon rupture → focal intimal injury at pin-hole rupture site → dissection entry [p.6]
- Post-IVL OCT 的 contrast injection → transient intracoronary pressure load (hydraulic stress) → IMH propagation from vulnerable entry [p.6]

#### Wall Integrity（組織學因素）
- IMH formation and expansion 需要 "relatively normal arc of arterial wall"（引用 IVUS studies, Ref 9）[p.6]
- Severe calcification / large plaques 後方的 media 通常 scarred and thinned → 作為 barrier limiting dissection propagation [p.6]
- 本案：rupture site **distal** 處有 severe circumferential calcification → scarred, noncompliant media → physically prevented antegrade splitting [p.6]
- Proximal LAD and LCX 具有 relatively preserved medial layer → compliant → 提供 hematoma expansion 的 substrate [p.6]

#### Geometric Steering（幾何因素）
- 引用 computational fluid dynamics studies (Ref 10)：coronary dissections characterized by segments of high curvature and torsion [p.6]
- LM → LCX 的 anatomical angulation 製造 geometric stress zone，不同於 straighter LAD [p.6]
- Hematoma 到達 LM bifurcation 後沿 curvature 轉向 LCX，而非進入 straighter, stiffer LAD [p.6]
- 作者稱此為 **"compliance mismatch" + "geometric steering"** 的組合效應 [p.6]

## OCT / IVUS Findings

### OCT（dissection 發生後）
- Immediately distal to guiding catheter in LM：no dissection → 支持 retrograde（而非 antegrade）propagation [p.4, Figure 2A]
- LM bifurcation：dissection extends into LCX ostium [p.4, Figure 2B]
- IVL balloon rupture site：entry tear identified [p.4, Figure 2C]
- 4 mm distal to rupture site：deep IVL-induced cracks within circumferential calcium **without dissection** [p.4, Figure 2D]

### IVUS（LCX assessment）
- Extensive intramural hematoma from LCX ostium to distal segment [p.3, p.5, Figure 3]
- 對應 angiography 上 distal LCX 的 filling defect [p.5, Figure 3]

---

## Key Lessons（Take-Home Messages）[p.1-2]

1. IVL balloon rupture 可在 **無明顯 vessel tortuosity 或 clearly demonstrable asymmetric balloon expansion** 情況下發生；eccentric calcification 可能是 contributing factor [p.2]
2. Dissection propagation 方向（antegrade vs retrograde）取決於 **"path of least resistance"**：distal calcification 可作為 barrier，forcing hematoma propagation retrogradely [p.2]
3. IVL balloon rupture 後 OCT imaging 具潛在風險：**contrast injection 可觸發 extensive dissection** [p.2]
4. After IVL rupture：**minimize contrast injection、avoid repeat OCT、reassess the entire coronary tree** [p.1]

- 強調 post-rupture OCT contrast injection 的 **hydraulic propagation** 風險
- 支持 **eccentric calcification** 作為 balloon rupture risk factor（即使無 severe tortuosity）
- 提供 practical recommendations：minimize contrast、avoid repeat OCT、reassess coronary tree
