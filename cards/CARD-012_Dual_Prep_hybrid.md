## 與本專案的關聯（IVL Balloon Complications）

### 核心發現

1. **IVL 在 atherectomy 後的安全性**: IVL 階段的 serious angiographic complications 僅 2.5%（3/120），全為 slow flow/no reflow；0 例 perforation、0 例 severe dissection、0 例 acute occlusion。相比之下，atherectomy 階段的 serious complications 高達 11.9% [p.5, p.7 Table 4]

2. **Hybrid approach 的安全性整體**: 30-day MACE-free rate 98.3%；periprocedural MI 1.7%（2 例）；0 例 cardiac death；0 例 TVR [p.6 Table 3]

3. **IVL balloon-specific complications 未個別報告**: 本文未獨立報告 IVL balloon rupture、balloon entrapment、balloon-related vessel injury。Complications 以 procedure phase（post-atherectomy / post-IVL / post-stent）分類 [p.7 Table 4]

4. **IVL crossing success 100%**: 所有病例在 atherectomy 後 IVL 均能成功通過病灶，証實 atherectomy-first 策略改善 IVL 通過性 [p.4, p.7]

5. **OCT-guided sizing 的角色**:
   - Mandatory OCT/OFDI 使整個流程有影像引導
   - OCT 用於決定是否需要追加 IVL（atherectomy 後 calcium score 仍 4.0 → 判斷追加 atherectomy 無效或危險 → 改用 IVL）
   - OCT 用於確認最終 stent expansion adequacy
   - 相較 Rota-Shock trial（IVUS 為主, imaging use 僅 ~50%），本研究的 mandatory OCT 可能貢獻於更佳安全性 [p.8–9]

6. **DES expansion 成果**: Mean stent expansion index 0.82 +/- 0.14 — 超過推薦閾值 0.8；eccentricity index 0.87 — 100% 患者 >0.7，表示均勻對稱擴張 [p.6, p.8 Table 5]

7. **Comparison with Rota-Shock**: 本研究 safety profile 優於 Rota-Shock registry（0 例 perforation vs Rota-Shock 有 perforation cases；IVL 後 reduced blood flow 更少見）；差異可能源於：(a) 本研究 100% elective IVL use vs Rota-Shock >50% bail-out use；(b) mandatory OCT guidance [p.8]

### 限制（與本專案相關）

- **Single-arm design**: 無法直接比較 atherectomy-only 或 IVL-only 策略的 complication rate
- **IVL balloon-specific AEs 未報告**: 無法從本文獲得 balloon rupture、balloon entrapment 等 IVL 裝置特異性併發症數據
- **僅 30 天追蹤**: 1-year results pending（已於 2026 另文發表 — PMID 41854787）
- **樣本量小** (n=118): 不足以偵測罕見併發症差異
- **Pre-PCI OCT 僅 56.7%**: 部分病例 imaging catheter 無法通過嚴重狹窄病灶 [p.10]
- **Selection bias**: 選擇 dual-prep 的病例本身即為 challenging cases（100% type B2/C, 100% severe calcification, 25.4% HD）[p.10]
- **RA/OA 選擇與 IVL sizing 由術者決定**: 未標準化 [p.10]

## Study Quality Notes

- **Sample size justification**: Based on analysis of prior real-world atherectomy studies; minimum 110 evaluable patients for 90% MACE-free rate with 95% CI 82.4–94.7% [p.4]
- **Core lab**: Independent angiographic and OCT/OFDI core lab (Micron, Inc., Osaka) [p.3]
- **Independent CEC**: All clinical events evaluated by independent Clinical Evaluation Committee [p.3]
- **MI definition**: SCAI definition for periprocedural MI + 4th Universal Definition for spontaneous MI [p.3]
- **Industry sponsorship**: Shockwave Medical funded study; involved in study design discussion but not data collection/analysis [p.10]
- **Open Access**: CC BY 4.0 license [p.10]
- **Japanese regulatory context**: This registry was specifically designed to support revision of IVL proper-use guidelines in Japan, permitting combined atherectomy + IVL use (previously restricted) [PMID 41335391 — Shiba et al., Cardiovasc Interv Ther 2025;41:29-35]
