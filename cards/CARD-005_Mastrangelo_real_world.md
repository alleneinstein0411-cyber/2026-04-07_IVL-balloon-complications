## 1 — 研究設計與方法

### 1.1 Population
- 收案期間共 4,191 patients / 7,540 lesions 接受 PCI；其中 105 (2.5%) patients / 110 (1.5%) calcified lesions 接受 IVL [p.2]
- 鈣化程度：moderate 16 (14.5%), severe 94 (85.5%) [p.6, Table 2]
- 四組分類 [p.2]：
  - **P-IVL (Primary):** 25 lesions — IVL 作為首選，不先用其他 balloon
  - **S-IVL (Secondary):** 51 lesions — NC balloon 擴張失敗後使用 IVL
  - **B-IVL (Bailout):** 11 lesions — stent 植入後因鈣化致 stent underexpansion，IVL 作為挽救
  - **ISR-IVL:** 23 lesions — ISR due to calcium-mediated stent under expansion

### 1.2 Endpoints
- **Primary effectiveness:** angiographic success = IVL balloon delivery + adequate stent expansion + residual DS <20% + final TIMI 3 flow [p.2-3]
- **Secondary effectiveness:** residual DS <50% [p.3]
- **Primary safety:** MACE (cardiac death + MI + TVR) — early (≤30 days) + late (>30 days) [p.3]
- **Secondary safety:** freedom from IVL-related procedural complications (flow-limiting dissection types D-F, slow/no-reflow, acute ST, perforation, malignant arrhythmias, IVL device failure) [p.3]

### 1.3 MI 定義
- 三種定義並行 [p.3]：
  1. CK-MB-based: post-PCI CK-MB peak >3x ULN
  2. Fourth Universal Definition
  3. SCAI definition

### 1.4 分析方法
- QCA: CAAS 8.2 Workstation (Pie Medical Imaging) [p.2]
- Paired t-test (baseline vs post-PCI QCA); chi-squared / Fisher's exact / ANOVA / Kruskal-Wallis for group comparisons [p.3]
- Logistic regression for TLR predictors [p.3]
- 統計軟體：Stata/SE 12.0 [p.3]

## 8 — TLR Predictors (Logistic Regression, Table 7) [p.9-10]

### 8.1 All TLR were clinically driven, all due to ISR [p.4]
- TLR distribution: P-IVL 12%, S-IVL 0%, B-IVL 0%, ISR-IVL 21.7% (p=0.002) [p.4]
- TLR at median 10.1 months (IQR 5-13.5) [p.10]

### 8.2 Multivariate Predictors [p.9-10, Table 7]

| Predictor | Beta | p |
|-----------|------|---|
| **ISR (calcium-mediated stent under expansion)** | **3.33** | **0.006** |
| **High-pressure predilatation before IVL (protective)** | **-2.12** | **0.04** |
| IVL-related procedural complications | 2.73 | 0.07 |
| Periprocedural MI (IV UD definition) | 2.62 | 0.08 |
| Periprocedural MI (SCAI definition) | -0.03 | 0.98 |

**重點：**
- ISR 為 TLR 最強預測因子 (beta=3.33, p=0.006) [p.10]
- 未先進行 high-pressure predilatation（即 P-IVL 策略）反而是 TLR 的獨立預測因子 (beta=-2.12, p=0.04) [p.10]
- 作者解釋此發現困難，推測 high-pressure dilatation 可能引發更強血管損傷與發炎反應 [p.10]

## 9 — Discussion 重點 [p.5, 7-10]

### 9.1 Primary vs Secondary IVL [p.7-8]
- P-IVL 與 S-IVL 在 baseline clinical/lesion/procedural characteristics 相似 [p.7]
- P-IVL 所需 balloons 數量顯著較少：1 (IQR 1-2) vs 3 (IQR 3-4); p=0.02 [p.8]
- P-IVL 較少需要 post-IVL pre-stent NC balloon: 36% vs 70.6%; p=0.006 [p.8]
- 作者推測真實世界中操作者傾向先嘗試傳統技術以降低成本 [p.8]

### 9.2 Bailout IVL [p.8]
- B-IVL 可在 stent underexpansion 時有效擴張，殘餘狹窄雖有趨勢性較高但未達統計顯著 [p.8]
- 深層鈣化時 plaque modification devices（壓縮/減積）可能失敗，IVL 藉多重 calcium cracks 可能更有效 [p.8]

### 9.3 ISR-IVL Group 長期結果不佳 [p.10]
- 儘管急性血管造影成功，long-term outcomes 在 ISR-IVL 組明顯較差 [p.10]
- MACE: 36.4% (ISR-IVL) vs 12.5% (P-IVL) vs 2.1% (S-IVL) vs 18.2% (B-IVL); p<0.001 [p.4]
- TLR: 21.7% (ISR-IVL)；definite ST 1 case + probable ST 1 case，均在 ISR-IVL 組 [p.4, 9]
- IVL for ISR 為 off-label 使用，即使 IVL + DCB 也無法作為最終治療方案 [p.10]
- 僅 47.8% ISR-IVL lesions 使用 intravascular imaging guidance → 可能部分解釋較差結果 [p.10]

### 9.4 Periprocedural MI Rate 較 Disrupt CAD III 高 [p.9-10]
- Fourth UD: 6.7% (本研究) vs 7.3% (Disrupt CAD III) — 相當 [p.10]
- CK-MB-based: 20% vs 6.8% — 高很多 [p.10]
- SCAI: 6.7% vs 2.6% — 較高 [p.10]
- 作者歸因於本研究患者冠脈病灶複雜度更高（包含 Disrupt CAD III 排除標準的患者：multivessel PCI, ISR, tortuous vessels, ostial lesions, LM disease, bifurcations, lesions >40mm, ACS）[p.10]

### 9.5 Complications vs Prior Studies [p.10]
- Flow-limiting dissection 2.7%、balloon burst 4.5%、perforation 0.9% — 與既有文獻一致 [p.10]
- 作者認為 IVL 不應在 unmonitored settings 使用 [p.10]
- TLR 7.3% 可與 rotational atherectomy 文獻比較 (TLR 8.2-16.6%) [p.10]

---

## 10 — Limitations [p.10-11]

1. Single-center, retrospective, observational — 可能不代表一般族群 [p.10]
2. IVL 用於不同 indications，部分為 off-label (B-IVL, ISR-IVL)；各組樣本量小 [p.10]
3. 無標準化 PCI 策略，操作者自行決定 [p.10]
4. IVUS/OCT 使用率低（整體 34.5%），尤其 ISR-IVL 組僅 47.8% [p.10]
5. 中位追蹤僅 12.9 個月 [p.10-11]
