## 1 — 研究設計與方法

### 1.1 Population
- All-comers international multicenter BENELUX-IVL registry (NCT06577038) [p.2]
- 年齡 >=18 歲，接受 PCI + IVL 治療 CAC 的患者 [p.2]
- 依併發症有無分組：complication (n=33) vs no complication (n=476) [p.2]

### 1.2 Endpoints
- **Primary:** 併發症發生率（coronary dissections, perforations, hemodynamic instability, abrupt vessel closure, slow/no reflow, resuscitation）[p.2]
- **Secondary:** residual diameter stenosis <30% on QCA; MACE (cardiac death, nonfatal target vessel MI, clinically driven TVR) at 1-year follow-up [p.2]

### 1.3 分析方法
- QCA 由 centralized core lab 以 Medis Suite 4.0.24.4 分析 [p.2]
- ICI 由 QCU-CMS 4.69 (Leiden UMC) 分析 [p.2]
- Kaplan-Meier + log-rank test 比較兩組存活 [p.2]
- Univariable + multivariable binary logistic regression（P<0.1 入選多變量）[p.2]
- 統計軟體：SPSS 25.0 [p.2]

### 1.4 Complication 分類
- 區分「immediately after IVL」vs「overall therapy (including concomitant therapy)」[p.2]
- 此分類經 centralized core lab 回溯冠脈造影驗證 [p.2]

## 5 — Clinical Outcomes (Table 4 & Figure 1) [p.4-5]

### 5.1 Procedural Success (QCA)
- Residual diameter stenosis <30%: **394/437 (90%)** overall [p.4]
  - Complication group: **28/29 (97%)** [p.4]
  - No complication group: 366/408 (90%) [p.4]
  - P = .341 [p.4]

### 5.2 Follow-up
- 1-year follow-up completed: 346/509 (68%) [p.4]
  - Complication group: 25/33 (76%) [p.4]
  - No complication group: 325/476 (68%) [p.4]

### 5.3 MACE (Table 4) [p.5]

| Time Period | Overall | No complication | Complication | P |
|-------------|---------|----------------|--------------|---|
| In-hospital MACE | 10 (2%) | 4 (1%) | 6 (18%) | <.001 |
| 30-Day MACE | 6 (1%) | 5 (1%) | 1 (3%) | .343 |
| 6-Month MACE | 15 (4%) | 14 (4%) | 1 (4%) | 1.000 |
| 1-Year MACE | 8 (2%) | 7 (2%) | 1 (5%) | .397 |
| **Cumulative 1-year MACE** | **39 (11%)** | **30 (9%)** | **9 (43%)** | **<.001** |

### 5.4 MACE 差異的驅動因素 [p.5]
- 差異主要由 **in-hospital events** 驅動 (P<.001) [p.4-5]
- In-hospital events breakdown:
  - Cardiac death: 8 total (5 in complication group, 3 in no-complication group) [p.5]
  - TVR: 2 total (1 in each group) [p.5]
- Cardiac death 原因 [p.5]：
  - Cardiogenic or hypovolemic shock: 5 cases
  - Sudden cardiac arrest: 2 cases
  - Iatrogenic aortic root dissection after high-pressure postdilatation: 1 case

### 5.5 Kaplan-Meier [p.5]
- 1-year survival probability significantly lower in complication group (log-rank P<.001) [p.4]
- 30-day MACE Kaplan-Meier: P=.007 [p.5, Figure 1]

## 8 — Limitations [p.6]

1. Retrospective observational design → 無法評估併發症發生機制 [p.6]
2. Complication 組樣本量小 (n=33) → 限制統計效能與亞組分析 [p.6]
3. ICI 非常規於 IVL 前後執行 → 無法評估 ICI 對預後影響 [p.6]
4. 結果應視為 hypothesis-generating [p.6]
